Artificial Genius Intelligence

Energy-Bound Discovery • Many Minds • Real Progress

Artificial Genius Intelligence (AGI²) is a new vision for machine intelligence: not one all-knowing system, but a network of slow-clock, energy-efficient specialist geniuses — each focused deeply on a single domain, working together to produce verified, high-impact discoveries.

Instead of measuring success by raw compute, AGI² is optimized for Joules per Discovery — how efficiently knowledge and progress emerge from the energy we invest.

This site will grow into a home for research papers, diagrams, and discussions on distributed genius architectures, neuromorphic design, and sustainable artificial creativity.
